Whose News

Ever wondered who writes your news? No, not the brand, but the corporations behind them? Like how Comcast owns The Verge (or other surprisingly suspicious ownerships). Whose News lets you know. Quickly, and right in your browser. With all sorts of features designed to let you know of potential conflict of intrests, you'll feel a bit safer reading the news.
